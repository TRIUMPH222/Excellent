BRIEF HISTORY

------------------   OF    --------------------

EXCELLENT ROCK SCHOOL

Excellent Rock School (ERS) was founded on November 6th, 2010 by the General Superintendent of the Deeper Christian Life Ministry, Pastor (Dr.) W. F. Kumuyi, whose passion for godliness, upright leadership and moral excellence is well known globally. DLHS is a faith-based, non-profit, e-learning facilitated and co-educational full boarding institution which provides a holistic education for all-round transformation and empowerment for excellence. The school’s motto, “Leadership with Distinction”, captures the vision of raising godly leaders. The vision statement of the school is “To transform our nation by producing upright leaders of the future through high quality and sound education” while her mission statement is “To produce students who are academically well-grounded, morally upright and adequately equipped as future leaders”.

DLHS started in Nigeria with seven campuses – Ado-Ekiti, Ibadan, Kaduna, Onitsha, Osogbo, Lagos and Port Harcourt. Over time, the number of campuses has grown to 23 in the states of Nigeria and the Federal Capital Territory.

PHILOSOPY

------------------   OF    --------------------

EXCELLENT ROCK SCHOOL

The school is committed to providing the highest academic standard, cultivating sound leadership skills and vision, as well as enriching lives through spiritual development. This aims at raising upright future leaders through the ideals and values of practical Christianity. Consequently, the school has zero tolerance for any act of indiscipline. In DLHS, there is no room for students to display affluence; the idea aims at giving a sense of equality to all students
